JANUARY

January's my month! It's not just about turning a year older, but also this month's packed with a lot of stuff going on. I've got my final exams for Semester 3, and let's just say it's a lot of stress, but I'm pushing through. And guess what? I also got to escape the grind for a bit and went on a trip to Pacitan—definitely one for the books, so many cool places to explore! On a scale of 1 to 10, I'd give January a solid 9/10. It's been a rollercoaster, but totally worth it!.
